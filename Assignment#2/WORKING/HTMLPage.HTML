Table of 10 HTML Tags:

HTML Tag Usage
p

Specifies the paragraph or text of any length. once p tag ends new line starts
br Used for New Line
b Bolds the text
u Underlines the text
marquee To have a moving text
small Makes the text smaller by 25%
mark Highlights the text in yellow
sub For subscript
hr For horizental line
q For putting Double Quotes
